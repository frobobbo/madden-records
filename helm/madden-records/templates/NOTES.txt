To add this Helm repo:
  helm repo add madden-records https://frobobbo.github.io/madden-records/charts
  helm repo update

To install:
  helm install madden-records madden-records/madden-records \
    --set postgresql.auth.password=<your-password>

To upgrade:
  helm upgrade madden-records madden-records/madden-records
